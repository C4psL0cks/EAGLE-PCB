  BYV 32-200 18A/200V

  NE 5819 3A/??V
  BSY 26-90 3A/90V
  SB 550 5A/50V
  SB 560* 5A/60V
  50 SQ 100 5A/100V
  MBR 745 7,5A/45V
  MBR 10100 10A/100V
  BSY 24-90 11A/90V
  MBR 1560 15A/60V
MBR 1645 16A/45V
  MYV 32-50 16A/50V
  MBR 2045 CT 20A/45V
  MBR 2090 20A/90V
  MBR 2545 CT 25A/45V

 This list is only a guide, these components can be substituted with
others. Pay attention to the max. current and max. voltage values.
:: As some of these diodes won't fit on the PCB,
you can mount them directly on the poles of the motor ::
