วงจรควบคุมเสต็ปเปอมอเตอร์(UNIPOLAR)1



คุณสมบัติของไอซี :
1.5A Maximum Output Current
35 V Output Sustaining voltage
Wave-Drive, Two-Phase, and Half Step Formats
Internal Clamp Diodes
Output enable control
Power on reset
Internal Thermal Shutdown
Sequence Connections Two-Phase Drive Sequence Pin 9 GND Pin 10 GND (Simplest choice)
Wave Drive Sequence Pin 9 5v Pin 10 GND
Half Step Drive Sequence Pin 9 GND Pin 10 5V
The resistors R1 & R2 are only necessary if the supply voltage to the motors is above 10 volts or so, and are really only necessary near max voltages and tuning the response times of the motor for high speeds
