เป็นแรงดัน RMS จะต้องคูณด้วย 0.707 อ่านค่าที่ DC volt-meter
( If the DVM or voltmeter has an input resistance of 10 megohms (most do these days), a voltage divider is formed by the series 4.7 meg resistor in the RF probe and the DMM resistance. This ratio is 10/(10+4.7) or .68 which is less than 5% from .707. For a simple circuit this is probably close enough!)
